## Why Use These Platforms?

- **Ease of use**: No-code/low-code options require minimal coding skills.
- **Rapid development**: Pre-built templates and tools speed up app creation.
- **Seamless deployment**: Integrated hosting and CI/CD pipelines simplify publishing.
- **Scalability**: Support for growing apps with robust backend integrations.
- **Community and support**: Access to tutorials, docs, and active communities.
